Stephanie Traudt photography

My name is Stephanie Traudt. I first started taking photos of building's and land in Omaha Nebraska. I enjoyed the small detials of old building's. I have recently started taking photos for family and friends. I consider myself lucky that I get to help capture these precious memories. I like to think of it as I get to creating art through my camera lens! My Goal is to help you frame your wonderful memories.
